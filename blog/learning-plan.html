Boot Camp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I am hoping to use the skills I learn at Dev Academy to obtain an entry-level job in the tech industry. I want to be able to build intuitive and modern websites for clients and work with an inspiring and progressive team. There I was to further my skills and become an experienced developer. Further down the track, I would like to move into working on application software for music, both for computer applications like digital audio workstations and for hardware instruments such as synthesisers. I want to rise to the level of a proficient and effective software developer and hope for a long and worthwhile career.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think my biggest limitation will be my learning difficulties. I struggle with dyslexia and it inhibits my ability to learn quickly meaning sometimes I need extra time to understand concepts. My strength is that I am determined to push and succeed and I know I love tech. When I have come to understand and write code so far it brings incredible happiness and this affirms me that I am on the right path. I think what I wrote in sprint twi is still true and I have come to understand more what is necessary for me when I am learning.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Staying in front, focusing and managing the workload and my time effectively will be the biggest challenges for me. Working on boosting my confidence and discussing work that I am very new to will also be a challenge, but I am excited to work on this and get better with time.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to work and improve my speaking and communication skills as much as I can. I would also like to boost my ability to work in a team with others effectively. I like working around other people and I see this as an incredibly valuable opportunity to learn and get feedback and peer programming experience.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

During boot camp, I want to be able to get support and technical guidance where I need it. I would also like to be given advice on where I can best improve and where I need to put in more work to further develop my skills in a positive way.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I know that I need to seek help quickly during boot camp. After foundation, I understand getting stuck and not seeking timely help is the worst thing as it means falling behind. My main goal for boot camp is to stay on top of the work and learn efficiently. I also need to make sure I balance my work and my life and take time to ensure I stay healthy physically and mentally. This will help support me while I study at Dev Academy and means I can show up and put my full self towards my learning. I will work to the best of my ability and make sure I communicate when I am in need of assistance and guidance. I will make sure I work respectfully and productively with my peers towards our common goal of succeeding and completing Dev Academy.
